Frequently Asked Questions

What is SmartLife Flexi?
SmartLife Flexi is a voluntary savings product from NSSF Uganda that lets you save towards personal goals at your own pace and schedule.
What is the minimum contribution?
The minimum contribution is UGX 5,000 per period. You can save daily, weekly, monthly, quarterly, semi-annually, or annually.
Do I need to be an NSSF member?
You can join NSSF as a voluntary member at any time. SmartLife Flexi is available to all voluntary members.
How is the 10% return calculated?
The 10% is an indicative annual return for demo purposes only. Actual returns are subject to NSSF fund performance and are not guaranteed.
How do I pay?
Payment methods include Mobile Money (MTN, Airtel), bank transfer, and employer payroll deductions.
